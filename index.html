About

We are a non emergency medical transportation provider committed to servicing the greater Metropolitan Detroit Area. Family owned and operated, we provide professional door-to-door transportation to all who require it.

We do not discriminate and accept all requests regardless of age, gender, ethnic, or religious affiliation.

To Know Us is to Trust Us!

  • All Major Insurances accepted
  • Sedan/minivan Transport
  • Excellent Safety Record
  • Clean, Comfortable Vehicles
  • Personal Injury Claims
  • Door-to-Door Service
  • Professional Courteous Drivers

Our rates are computed based on the following:

  1. One-way/Round Trip base cost
  2. Special equipment needs
  3. Waiting time
  4. Mileage cost, fixed for the first 40 miles
  5. Flat Rates for Attorney and Court Travels, (depending on Location)

Our Mission

We Strive to provide the most cost-effective and quality service possible to meet any client's level of mobility or state of health. Whether physically challenged or not, our care providers are dedicated to ensuring your safe transport. Including an On time pickup. BOTH too and from your appointments.

Our Assisted Transport Services include

  • Scheduled Medical appointments
  • Same-day appointments
  • Doctor's office appointments
  • Hospital discharge
  • Physical Therapy
  • Assisted living facilities
  • Dialysis
  • Rehabilitation
  • Attorney Appointments
  • Private citizens
  • Hearing Impaired

Policies

All service is curb to curb. Passengers are required to wear seatbelts. Eating, smoking, and drinking are not permitted in the vehicles. Our drivers may not accept tips, however donations are appreciated. Donation envelopes are available upon request.

What We Ask You To Do:

Be ready for your ride early since others may need to be picked up after you. Traffic conditions vary and delays may be unavoidable. We don't want you to miss any appointment! Please, be cooperative and courteous of Our Drivers and other passengers.

If you need to cancel your ride, please call us
